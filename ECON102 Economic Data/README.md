## Business Task
Create and use time series visualizations to analyze consumption expenditure, annualized growth rate, time series decomposition, comovement, cost of living, and business cycle, growth and inequality.

## About Assignment #2

- Datasets: Nominal wages for males and females and CPI from 1997 to 2019 in Quebec, Transportation and Warehousing Industry from [wage64.csv](https://github.com/itsmichelletrinh/Excel/blob/main/ECON102%20Economic%20Data/Assignment%20%232/Wage64.csv) and country real GDP from 1970 to 2017 from [realgdp.csv](https://github.com/itsmichelletrinh/Excel/blob/main/ECON102%20Economic%20Data/Assignment%20%232/realgdp.csv).

    between the years 1982 and 2007.
